Meet the Music Director

Kelechi Nnam

Kelechi Nnam is a trained Engineer and a practicing Business Development Expert with a flair for good music. A counter Tenor of high repute, songwriter and worship leader with interests in music composition and choral conducting. He joined the Laz Ekwueme Chorale in 2005 where he got most of his training from HRH Igwe Prof Laz Ekwueme. He holds a diploma in music and is currently the music Director of The Chorus.
He has served as Choirmaster and Music Director in various Anglican and Methodist Churches in Nigeria.

He is currently the Deputy Choirmaster of St. Matthew's Anglican Church Maitama, and is married to Chidera.

Meet the Accompanists

Iheanyi Owutu

Iheanyi Owutu Started his musical journey as a soprano singer at St Peters Anglican Church, Karu, Abuja, where he also served as the church organist.
Born into a music family, he grew through the 'ranks' in the choir: Soprao-Alto-Tenor - Bass (Bass unofficial). It is safe to say he is a Tenor.
The Abia State indigene holds a degree in Sociology/Anthropology from Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka.
He served as the Organist and one time choir master of the Anglican Chapel (temporal and permanent sites) Chapel of Redemption - Nnamdi Azikiwe University, Awka; Organist at St Marks Anglican Church, Emene, Enugu; St Michael and All Angels Anglican Church, Ifite Awka, Anambra state etc. He is presently the organist of the Basilica of Grace, Gudu, Abuja.
He is one of the accompanists of THE CHORUS, amongst others.

Lawal Omeiza Godwin

Lawal Omeiza Godwin is a pianist who also holds a degree in Building Technology. He has performed several piano solos and choral works by Nigerian and Western composers from different musical eras. He is also a fine organist. His passion for new skills and acquiring knowledge led to his discovery and love for music.
Lawal has a Diploma in Church Music Performance and has gained a lot of experience serving as a Pianist/Organist in various capacities in different parts of Nigeria.
He served as an organist and director of music at the St. John's Anglican Church, Ohueta, Diocese of Okene, an Organist at the Independent Baptist Church, Tunga, Minna, Niger state, as a visiting organist at the Anglican Church of the Pentecost, Mabushi, and St Matthew's Anglican Church Maitama, both located in Abuja. He is also the assistant accompanist/pianist for the prestigious Minna Choral society, was the resident Pianist for the Rising star recital series project in 2021 for the Nigerian philharmonic company, Abuja, is currently a member of the Festival Of Praise Choir And Orchestra (FOPCAO), Abuja, Nigeria, and one of the accompanists of The Chorus.

Meet the Sopranos

Nengimonyu Susan Okara (Mrs)

Meet Nengimonyu Susan Okara (Mrs), a geologist and soprano singer with The Chorus, Abuja.

She started singing at a tender age in her home church, St. Luke's Cathedral, Nembe. She is a member of various choral groups in Abuja where she currently resides and also a chorister of The Anglican church of the Pentecost, Mabushi, Abuja. Biebele Friday Eretoru

Biebele Joy Eretoru is a trained Plant Biotechnologist, Educationist and Fashion designer currently working as a Fashionpreneur and a Development practitioner with a passion for good Church/gospel music.
She doubles as an Alto and a mezzo-Soprano and is also a contemporary gospel worship leader.
She has served as a Chorister and a member of the gospel praise/worship team in different churches in South-southern and south-western Nigeria.
She joined the wood-wind ensemble of God's own choir (Bethel Anglican Church Amadi-Ama) in 2008 where she got most of her training in classical music, contemporary and in the recorder instrument. She later joined the Harmonic ensemble in 2014, and then the Fused Ensemble Port Harcourt in 2017, where she was mentored in Music by Dr. Precious Omuku who was also the Director of Music.
She is the Creative Director of BJ's KORNA, a Start-up sustainable fashion brand, and the founder of ORFSKAP INITIATIVE. An initiative for Orphans where she takes sustainable and ethical fashion skills training to the orphanage, training teenagers and young adults in the vocation.

Tochukwu Uche

Tochukwu is a from Abia State, a multipurpose chorister. He holds a degree in Accounting, and is also a Company Incorporation Consultant, a computer software and hardware consultant, and a Frontend Web Developer.
He was born into a family of choristers and music lovers which made his relationship with music "Love at first sight".
He started his music career at a tender age and was quickly exposed to both classical and contemporary music. He started out as a tenor and later went on to sing soprano, sang some bass solos and finally settled for the Alto part. He started his music journey at St. Peters Anglican Church, Kano, under the guidance of Late Meastro Godfrey Osondu. He was also a member of St. Nicholas Anglican church Choir, Aluu, Port Harcourt, University of Port Harcourt Choir, St. Barthlomew Cathedral Choir, The Blue Notes, Abuja Choral society and Festival of Praise Choir.
He has a vast knowledge of classical music and composers, and is also a Collector of music. Some refer to him as "The Archive" due to his vast library of classical music.
His hobbies are singing, research, coding, traveling and making friends.
