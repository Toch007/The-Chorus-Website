What's The Chorus?

The chorus is simply a group of Choral Music lovers who decided to come together to take Choral Music in Abuja and Nigeria to a whole new level.
A harmony in unison, talents from different parts of the country, of various backgrounds and walks of life.

Our Previous Performances

Are you having difficulties getting the sheet music of any of our previous performances? Easy!
Just send a mail to scores@thechorusabuja.org or visit the Music Score page and fill the Music Fequest Form.

You can make a special request to us for a music score you would love to have. We may not have all the music in the world, but we might have just what you are looking for. You can send your special request to scores@thechorusabuja.org or visit the Music Score page and fill the Music Fequest Form.

Are you a composer?
Do you have properly composed pieces?
Are you seeking ways to promote your music?
Do you have a choir that can give your music the quality it deserves?
Write us today and take your composition(s) to the next level. Send your mail to composer@thechorusabuja.org or visit the Music Score page and fill the Music Composer Form.

Join Us

Are you a choral musician?
Are you seeking to improve your singing?
Are you a Christian?
Do you enjoy Musical challenges?
Do you want to be part of a great Music Community?

As a music community willing to grow and improve others, we are open to new members.

We hold auditions twice a year.

If you are interested in joining us, please visit the About page, download and fill the Membership Application Form.

You can choose to be a non-perfoming or simply a supporting member, or maybe even a sponsor of The Chorus by visiting our Donate page. You can also send a mail to us at support@thechorusabuja.org
